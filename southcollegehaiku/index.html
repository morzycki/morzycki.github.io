South College Haiku

the big wheel chair ramp
it would be useful, but it
leads to a staircase

particle of dust
flaky, white, not quite normal
maybe asbestos?

dusty South College
ancient, and never maintained
there, squirrels abound

hallways lead nowhere
fuck if I know where to go
or how to get out

that dead undergrad?
didn’t ask for directions
fell out the window

the book on my desk
didn't look like this before
nibbled by squirrels

linguists in the front
dean beneath, comp lit in back
rodents on the roof

a dry hacking cough,
itchy eyes, nonstop sneezing
South College-itis

smells like an attic
so badly ventilated
and vaguely like feet

For the uninitiated: Originally built in the 19th century as part of a fraternity prank, South College became home to the UMass linguistics department in the early 1970s. It was last vacuumed in the mid ’80s. Definite descriptions of its elevators (Heim & Kratzer 1998) induce failure of an existence presupposition, but definite descriptions of a mysterious underground tunnel connecting it to the main library building do not. The building features structural amenities such as deteriorated sound-proof walls and squirrels. Portions of it have been condemned or have fallen on Barbara Partee (both of these are literally true).
