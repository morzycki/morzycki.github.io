Morzycki, Marcin. 2010. ‘Quantification Galore’. Appeared in 2011 in Linguistic Inquiry 42(4): 671–682. A pre-publication draft is here. BibTeX

This squib examines the grammar of galore, arguing that it is a quantificational determiner that operates exclusively on kinds. In doing so it realizes a possible determiner denotation that one might independently expect to occur in English in the crosslinguistic framework proposed in Chierchia (1998).
